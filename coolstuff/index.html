Media I really like and you should too

Here I want to discuss some interesting things you should look into

The Sopranos

So, I just finished The Sopranos and I loved it. So cool. Such a cool ending. It was very thought provoking and subverted my expectations. The prequel, The Many Saints of Newark, came out recently, but I did not really like it. The characters lacked the complexity that made them so interesting on the show. Very much just felt like "hey, remember The Sopranos?". This is not how I want to remember it.

Stop Making Sense

I love this album/movie. It is a live performance by The Talking Heads. It is so energetic, fun, and weird. Nearly every song on here is better than their studio version counter-parts. There is a life present in these live versions that the studio versions can't match.
